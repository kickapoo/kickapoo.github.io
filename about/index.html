I am who I am, and I am just another human

Hey, people of the internet and the world, meet me and me is Stavros from Greece, currently living in Sweden.

I work as Software Developer and HR managers might entitle me as Full Stack Developer. Most the time, I am using python, but I am all over the place.

It did not start like this…+

I always wanted to be something related to Sea but during my Greek exams for University I was only able to get in “Forestry” Department.
After that, all my master thesis and experience was related around water, and more specific in awesome field Hydrology and Hydrological modelling. I went further to learn Fuzzy Logic and many mathematical and statistical modelling patterns (a whole chapter in my life of 5 year in the beautiful island of Samos, Aegean Sea with the local University).

But, Greek culture, Greek system and Greek economic crisis don’t really care about Environmental Sciences in like other European Countries.

I need to make something for myself and support my new family, so I became a self touch programmer. Lot of late night readings, lot of frustration, lot of new things but I had patience and was detrimented.

So…

The last 10 years (now 2020) I work as a programmer and still am.

but yet another blog

I want to start document and share my “shit” like all many others go.

That ever will be written here, will be out of my heart and my poor logic with respect.

No one knows everything and no one knows nothing.

My writing style, my writing skills might be usefull to someone out there.
